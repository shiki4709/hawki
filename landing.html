Lead Discovery

Hawki

Find leads who engage with content in your space. Scrape LinkedIn post engagers, filter by ICP, draft personalized messages with AI.

Get Hawki

LinkedIn post scraping

Paste any LinkedIn post URL. Hawki extracts every liker and commenter with their name, headline, and company. 1,000+ leads per post.

ICP filtering

Set your target titles (AE, SDR, VP Sales). Leads are sorted by engagement quality: ICP commenters first, then ICP likers, then everyone else.

AI message drafting

Claude AI drafts personalized connection messages that reference the post and their comment. Edit with natural language: "make it shorter", "mention their company".

Pipeline tracking

Track every lead from scrape to signup. Message tracking auto-updates your pipeline: scraped, ICP, messaged, replied, signed up.

Influencer watch list

Monitor thought leaders in your space. Check all their recent posts with one click. Track which posts you've already scraped.

CSV export

Export all leads or ICP-only leads as CSV. Import into your CRM, Dripify, or any outreach tool.
